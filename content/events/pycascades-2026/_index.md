PyCascades is a regional PyCon in the Pacific Northwest, celebrating the west coast Python developer and user community.

Rodrigo Silva Ferreira from Posit will be presenting "To Notebook or Not to Notebook: Multilingual Workflows for Exploratory Data Analysis", where he will explore how multilingual workflows in notebooks can transform exploratory data analysis (while also introducing new challenges).
