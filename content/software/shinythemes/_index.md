Give your Shiny applications a professional, polished look with shinythemes, a package that brings popular Bootstrap themes from Bootswatch directly to R. With just a single line of code, you can transform your application's appearance by choosing from 16 carefully crafted themes, ranging from clean and minimal designs like Flatly and Simplex to bold, distinctive styles like Cyborg and Superhero. Whether you're building internal dashboards or user-facing applications, shinythemes makes it effortless to create visually appealing interfaces that match your project's aesthetic.

The package includes a live theme selector that lets you preview different themes in real-time without reloading your application, making it easy to experiment and find the perfect design. Simply add `themeSelector()` to your UI during development, test various options interactively, and then commit to your favorite theme with a single parameter. Note that shinythemes has been superseded by the bslib package, which offers additional features and support for Bootstrap 4, but shinythemes remains a simple and effective solution for quickly styling Bootstrap 3-based Shiny applications.
