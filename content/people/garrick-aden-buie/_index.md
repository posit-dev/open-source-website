Garrick Aden-Buie is a Software Engineer for [Shiny]({{<ref "software/shiny">}}) at Posit. He builds broadly accessible tools that help everyone do data science in R and Python with Shiny, Quarto and R Markdown. Before Shiny, he helped build RStudio Academy. Garrick is a passionate and long-time R user and educator and has enjoyed sharing his work online at [https://garrickadenbuie.com](https://garrickadenbuie.com) and via numerous open source projects including [regexplain](https://www.garrickadenbuie.com/project/regexplain/), [xaringanExtra](https://pkg.garrickadenbuie.com/xaringanExtra), and [epoxy](https://pkg.garrickadenbuie.com/epoxy).
