I'm Emil Hvitfeldt, a Senior Software Engineer at Posit on the [tidymodels]({{<ref "software/tidymodels" >}}) team. One of my main areas of interest is feature engineering and preprocessing, particularly the space around [recipes]({{<ref "software/recipes" >}}) and its extensions like [textrecipes]({{<ref "software/textrecipes" >}}) and [embed]({{<ref "software/embed" >}}). I also work on [orbital]({{<ref "software/orbital" >}}), which lets you take tidymodels workflows and deploy them without R dependencies. Outside of modeling, I enjoy pushing the boundaries of what's possible with slide-making in [Quarto]({{<ref "software/quarto" >}}).
