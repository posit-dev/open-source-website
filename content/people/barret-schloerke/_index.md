`hello()`! Dr. Barret Schloerke is a Senior Software Engineer at Posit who has spent nearly two decades convincing computers to make data beautiful, interactive, and well tested — preferably all three at once. As a core contributor to Shiny, he has helped shape [`{shiny}`](https://shiny.posit.co/r/), [`{shinytest2}`](https://rstudio.github.io/shinytest2/), [`{reactlog}`](https://rstudio.github.io/reactlog/), and [`{plumber}`](https://www.rplumber.io/) on the R side, extended that work into [Shiny for Python](https://shiny.posit.co/py/) with its testing infrastructure, bookmarking, and interactive data frame experience, and most recently brought built-in [OpenTelemetry](https://opentelemetry.io/) support for production observability to both platforms.

Beyond Shiny, his open-source portfolio spans AI tooling ([`{ellmer}`](https://ellmer.tidyverse.org/), [`{shinychat}`](https://posit-dev.github.io/shinychat/), [`{querychat}`](https://posit-dev.github.io/querychat/)), data visualization ([`{GGally}`](https://ggobi.github.io/ggally/), [`{trelliscopejs}`](https://hafen.github.io/trelliscopejs/)), and API tooling ([`{gqlr}`](https://schloerke.com/gqlr/)).
