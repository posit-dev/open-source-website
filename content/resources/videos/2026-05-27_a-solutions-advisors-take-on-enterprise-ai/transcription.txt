<h3>AI productivity and agents</h3>

<p><a data-t="39">They're delivering really good value</a> <a data-t="40">in terms of speeding up your productivity,</a> <a data-t="43">being able to automatically code,</a> <a data-t="45">being able to generate things</a> <a data-t="47">that have a lot of that high frequency.</a> <a data-t="49">And when you start thinking about agents,</a> <a data-t="53">it's not so much, can an agent do this?</a> <a data-t="56">Where it falls apart is fitting that</a> <a data-t="58">into your actual environment.</a></p>

<h3>Treating automation as a spectrum</h3>

<p><a data-t="74">The organizations that are really successful with that</a> <a data-t="77">are treating it more like a spectrum</a> <a data-t="79">rather than this kind of binary automate everything</a> <a data-t="82">or just do manual processes.</a> <a data-t="84">So they're really looking at it</a> <a data-t="86">from two different decisions.</a> <a data-t="88">One, do I have this process</a> <a data-t="89">that has high frequency and low risk?</a> <a data-t="92">Yes, let's automate this.</a></p>

<h3>Scaling data science as an organizational product</h3>

<p><a data-t="116">It's not seeing your data science and AI organizations</a> <a data-t="120">as kind of like this experimentation kitchen.</a> <a data-t="123">It's really embracing AI and data</a> <a data-t="126">as an organizational product,</a> <a data-t="130">embedding that into your workflows</a> <a data-t="132">and essentially making that a repeatable process.</a></p>

<p><a data-t="135">It might be one thing to take a model</a> <a data-t="137">and be able to put it into production,</a> <a data-t="139">but how easy is it for you</a> <a data-t="141">to take more of your data science workflows</a> <a data-t="143">and spread that across the organization and really scale?</a> <a data-t="146">And unless those organizations are really looking at it</a> <a data-t="149">from a holistic view,</a> <a data-t="151">you're always going to be stuck in experimenting,</a> <a data-t="154">prototyping, and not really getting things</a> <a data-t="156">to that production level.</a></p>
