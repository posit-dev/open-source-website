  This week''s guests were Rachael Dempsey, Customer Marketing Lead at Posit, and Libby Heeren, Data Community Manager at Posit!


  Some topics covered in this week''s Hangout were community building strategies, return on investment for community events, career transitions into data science and community management, and advice for starting internal communities.


  One community member asked: "What are challenges that you face being a community builder, and what are tips you would give to folks trying to build community as well?"


  Libby answered: The biggest challenge is that people don''t willingly volunteer to do things when you ask a group for volunteers. The most important tip is to individually ask people to contribute rather than making broad requests. You need someone who is consistently inviting, welcoming, and scheduling. Find a balance between structure (consistent schedule, same time and place) and flexibility (don''t over-prescribe the meeting content). Make it convenient for yourself as the organizer since you''ll be the one who always shows up.
