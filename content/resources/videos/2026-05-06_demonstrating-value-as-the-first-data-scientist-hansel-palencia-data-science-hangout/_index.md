  This week''s guest was Hansel Palencia, Manager of Data Science at DaVita International!


  Some topics covered in this week''s Hangout were clinical data workflows and data governance, building data science teams from scratch in healthcare, predictive modeling for patient outcomes, and university major transitions from pre-med ambitions to discovering data science.


  One community member asked: What does the data show predicts healthy kidneys? (DaVita is a company centered on providing dialysis, a treatment for kidney failure)


  Hansel answered: Exercise and water are the two biggest things that you can do to prevent kidney disease. Exercise helps manage things like hypertension and obesity, which are major causes of kidney disease. Water and fluid intake are also key. Diet and exercise are your major influencers of whether you have kidney disease or not.
