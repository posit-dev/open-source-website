We're excited to launch the Posit Open Source community hub, a central place to discover and engage with our open source projects, contributors, and community.

## What You'll Find Here

This site serves as a comprehensive resource for:

- **Projects**: Explore our open source packages and tools
- **People**: Meet the developers and contributors behind our projects
- **Events**: Stay updated on conferences, meetups, and community gatherings
- **Resources**: Access documentation, tutorials, and learning materials

## Our Commitment to Open Source

At Posit, we believe in the power of open source software to advance data science and scientific computing. Our projects are built on collaboration, transparency, and community-driven innovation.

## Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned contributor or just getting started, there are many ways to participate:

- Contribute code or documentation
- Report issues and suggest features
- Share your projects and experiences
- Join our community discussions

We look forward to building together!
